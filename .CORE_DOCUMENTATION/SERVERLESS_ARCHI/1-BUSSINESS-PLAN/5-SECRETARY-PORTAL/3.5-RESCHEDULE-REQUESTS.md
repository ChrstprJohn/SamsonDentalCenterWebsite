# Secretary Portal: Reschedule Requests Approval

**Route**: `/secretary/reschedule-requests`

This page is a dedicated queue for reviewing, approving, or rejecting reschedule requests submitted by authenticated patients for their already approved appointments.

## 1. Split-Pane Layout
The page uses a 2-column layout to allow quick comparisons and state updates:
- **Left Column (Requests Table)**: A list of all active reschedule requests (`status = 'RESCHEDULE_REQUESTED'`). Clicking a row selects it and loads its details on the right pane.
- **Right Column (Details Pane)**: Displays the comparison of the original appointment details vs. the proposed reschedule details, the patient's explanation, dentist daily schedule, and action controls.

## 2. Left Column (Requests Table)
Lists individual reschedule requests.

### Columns
- **Patient**: Patient name (shows dependent name if applicable).
- **Service**: Requested treatment/service.
- **Proposed Date & Time**: Target date and time slot proposed by the patient.

---

## 3. Right Column (Details Pane)
Shows a side-by-side or block comparison of the original slot vs. the proposed slot.

### 3.1 Comparison Layout
* **Original Appointment Block**:
  * Date, start/end time
  * Assigned Doctor
* **Proposed Reschedule Block** (Highlighted):
  * Proposed Date, start/end time
  * Proposed Doctor
* **Patient Justification**:
  * Displays the reason entered by the patient for the reschedule.

### 3.2 Doctor Daily Schedule Timeline
A timeline of the proposed doctor's slots on the proposed date (8:00 AM – 4:30 PM) is shown to help the secretary verify that the slot is open and free of conflicts before approving.

---

## 4. Decision Actions
At the bottom of the details pane, the secretary configures and commits the decision:
- **Decision Selection** (Button Group):
  * **Approve**: Swaps proposed date/time/doctor into the actual appointment fields and sets status to `APPROVED`.
  * **Reject**: Reverts status to `APPROVED` and retains original date/time/doctor.
- **Reason/Remarks**: Dropdown selector + custom text area to record reasons for audit trail.
- **Confirm**: Commits the action via the unified transaction.
